Our Story

In pictures

Alli and Alex met at Virginia Tech in the summer of 2008. They spent the summer evenings at Mill Mountain, the local coffee shop, getting to know each other while working on fun, dorky projects. Over that and the next summer, they fell in love and have been together since the winter of 2009. The rest, as they say, is history...

Georgetown

Engagement

Alex proposed to Alli in Washington, DC on December 20, 2014. Alli had no idea what a completely special night he planned, thinking they were just going ice skating with some friends. Before he got down on one knee, Alex asked "How long would you like to be together?" Alli answered with "Forever." They will make that come true this July!
